Practical Assessment

When I first approached the practical assessment, I was both excited and apprehensive. Stick welding, known for its versatility and strength, requires a steady hand and keen attention to detail. The initial phases of the assessment were challenging. Holding the electrode at the correct angle, maintaining a consistent arc length, and controlling the weld pool were skills that demanded both patience and practice. Despite the initial difficulties, I was determined to improve. I realized that welding is not just about joining metals; it’s about understanding the behavior of materials under heat, the properties of different electrodes, and the importance of safety protocols. This comprehensive learning experience significantly broadened my technical knowledge and skill set.
